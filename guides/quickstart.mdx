**1. Get your API Credentials**

MagicFurnish provides you with:

- **API Key**
- **API Secret**

Include these headers with every request.

\> See: [Authentication](../api/authentication)

**2. Upload a Floor Plan**

Send a JPG, PNG, or single-page PDF to the Upload endpoint.

\`\`\`bash

curl -X POST "https://api.magicfurnish.com/api/v1/layouts/process" \\

-H "X-API-Key: YOUR_API_KEY" \\

-H "X-API-Secret: YOUR_API_SECRET" \\

-F "image=@/path/to/floorplan.png"
